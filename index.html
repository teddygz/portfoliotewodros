About Me

I am from Ethiopia and a student of Master of Science in the University of Washington-Tacoma. I completed an undergraduate and masters degree in Ethiopia with GIS and Worked for a Government Organization in different areas application of interest.

Experiance and Skill

I am experienced in various types of expertise area as a "GIS, Remote Sensing, Photogrammetrist, Aerial Surveyor and Imagery Analyst"

My experience is ranging from mapping and analyzing geospatial data, hydro breakline collection, lidar manual classification using TerraScan and TerraModeler, Aerial photo collection using Microsoft's Ultracam Eagle Aerial Sensor, processing Images from Aerial Triangulation up to Orthophoto Production using Inpho photogrammetric suite and image Classification.
